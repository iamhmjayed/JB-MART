About JB MART

Founded in 2025, JB MART is your friendly neighborhood grocery store committed to providing fresh, high-quality products with exceptional customer service. Our mission is to make grocery shopping convenient, affordable, and enjoyable for everyone in our community.

We source our products from local farmers and trusted suppliers to ensure you get the freshest produce, dairy, and pantry staples. Our team works tirelessly to maintain the highest standards in product quality and customer satisfaction.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery hours?

We deliver daily from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM. You can select your preferred delivery time at checkout.

Do you offer same-day delivery?

Yes! Orders placed before 12:00 PM will be delivered the same day. Orders after 12:00 PM will be delivered the next day.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns of non-perishable items within 7 days with receipt. For perishable items, please contact us immediately if there are any issues.

Still have questions?

If you didn't find the answer you were looking for, feel free to contact our customer support team. We're here to help you with any questions or concerns you may have about our products or services.
